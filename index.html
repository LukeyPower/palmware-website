Croc Hunter

Croc Hunter is a classic arcade rail shooter developed for the 2026 GeekFest game jam in Darwin, NT. Set on croc-infested Top End waters, players blast their way through waves of crocodiles from a moving boat and fight to survive until daybreak — capped off by a showdown with Big Croc himself.

Built in Unity — developed for GeekFest 2026, Darwin NT.
